"I think, therefore I am"

The Latin cogito, ergo sum, usually translated into English as "I think, therefore I am", is the "first principle" of the philosophy of the French scientist and philosopher René Descartes. "Socrates"

TSocrates ( 470–399 BC) was a foundational Greek philosopher from Athens, credited as a founder of Western philosophy and the first moral philosopher. Known for his "Socratic method" of questioning, he focused on ethics and self-examination, famously claiming the unexamined life is not worth living. He left no writings, with his life and ideas known through students, mainly Plato.
